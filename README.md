# Controls
Move player - WASD

Move camera - Mouse

Kick (when near ball) - Left Click Mouse Button

Use Boost - Q

# How to Play
Adjust settings such as volume or player/enemy movement speed in the Settings menu. Press Start to play once you're ready.

On the whistle, try to score against the enemy AI by kicking the ball toward the enemy goal. The blue squares on the field are speed boosts that can help you win. The enemy will be able to kick the ball away from you and toward your goal if it gets close enough.

First to 3 points wins the game. You can return to the Main Menu from there or exit.
